 General Overview

When can I start? Fall (September), Winter (January), Spring (April), or Summer (July)

How long is the program? 1 quarter (2 or 3 months) with 18 total credits

When do classes meet? Time varies but all options are open for in-person, hybrid, and online.

In this certificate program, you prepare for entry-level careers in office occupations. You will be provided hands-on training with computers on Microsoft Office applications, business math, business communication, career readiness, human relations, and general clerical skills.

Many students take Office Assistant classes to get ready for another program that uses computers frequently, like Cloud Network Technology, Computer Science, Construction Management, or Medical Office Programs.

You also can use ASST classes as electives for an AAS degree in Accounting, Contemporary Business, or Legal Assistant.

 Financial Aid Eligibility Warning

This specific program is not eligible for federal financial aid because it is only one quarter long. Please talk to your adviser about different ways to pay for tuition.
